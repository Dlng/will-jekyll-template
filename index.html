Mission

Industries need automation. Globally, over 800 million manufacturing jobs still involve repetitive routines or even work hazards. Roboeye aims to provide industrial automation solutions that are robust and affordable for manufacturing.

We are a team of passionate researchers and engineers in robotics and artificial intelligence. At RoboEye, we are developing cutting-edge technologies in machine learning, reinforcement learning and deep learning. Our product is a general-purpose cognitive platform for robotics that can adapt to user-defined tasks.
